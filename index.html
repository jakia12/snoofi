How to buy $SNOOFI

Make Wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

GET SOME ETH

have ETH in your wallet to switch to $SNOOFI. If you dont have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

BUY $SNOOFI

Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $SNOOFI token address into Uniswap, select $SNOOFI and confirm.
